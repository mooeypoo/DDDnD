# Reading the Audit Report

The audit report provides data-driven insights into scenario balance, fairness, and content health.

## Audit Findings

Findings are categorized by severity:

- **Critical** - Severe balance or fairness issues that should be fixed
- **Warning** - Moderate issues that may need attention
- **Info** - Informational findings for context

## Categories

Findings fall into several categories:

- **Scenario Difficulty** - Win rate and outcome distribution
- **Score Balance** - Distribution of score metrics across players
- **Stakeholder Balance** - Fairness of stakeholder effects
- **Rule Triggers** - Frequency and impact of rule triggers
